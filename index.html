Adopt-a-Family 2022 was a resounding success!
For the first time in the history of the Adopt-a-Family program, every registered family was adopted by a neighbor. Our community pulled together to help 613 families with 1540 children celebrate the holidays with gifts for the children. Your generosity has made lifelong happy memories possible for so many families: from learning to ride a bike to having a warm coat to play outside, your gifts immediately impact our community's children in a positive, profound way. From all of us at CAC - thank you for making the holidays magical!

VITA Program on track to get clients over $250,000 in total tax returns
CAC's participation in the IRS's VITA (Volunteer Income Tax Assistance) program netted our clients $277,639 in tax refunds during the 2021 tax season - and we're ready to do it again!! Appointments will start the last week of January. Questions? Contact us at vita@ourcac.org.
Get your taxes done (or tell a friend!)
If your household income is less than $60,000 and you reside in Georgia, you qualify for CAC's VITA program! Appointments will start the last week of January on Mondays, Wednesdays and Saturdays. Once you have an appointment, the VITA team will contact you about locations, times, and required documents.
